DALL-E 3, is here to stay.

September 15, 2023 Isaac Castillo

DALL·E 3 is the latest version of the text-to-image system created by OpenAI. This system is focused on understanding more nuances and details than previous systems, which allows you to easily translate your ideas into exceptionally more accurate images.

Image generated by DALL·E 3
Improvements over DALL·E 2

Unlike modern text-to-image systems that tend to ignore words or descriptions, DALL·E 3 represents a leap forward in the ability to generate images that follow the letter of the text you enter.

DALL·E 3 and ChatGPT: A powerful combination

DALL·E 3 is built natively on ChatGPT, which allows you to use ChatGPT as a partner for brainstorming and refining your texts. When given an idea, ChatGPT will automatically generate detailed texts for DALL·E 3 that will capture your idea in images.

Focus on safety and creative control

As previous versions, measures have been taken to limit the ability of DALL·E 3 to generate violent, adult or hateful content. In addition, DALL·E 3 has measures to reject requests that ask for a public figure by name or a style of a living artist. It is also being investigated the best way to help people identify when an image was created with AI.

Conclusion

DALL·E 3 is an exciting breakthrough in generating images from text. With its integration with ChatGPT and its focus on safety and creative control, it promises to be a valuable tool for content creators.
