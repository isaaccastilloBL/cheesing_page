1. Collection of Personal Data:

When using our website and completing our contact forms, we may collect certain personal data that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and any other information you provide in the message.

We will use the personal data you provide exclusively for contact and communication purposes related to the queries, questions, or requests you have submitted through our contact forms.

3. Storage and Deletion of Data:

The personal data collected will be securely stored in our records for a period of three (3) months. After this period, the data will be securely and permanently deleted, ensuring the privacy and protection of your information.

Cheesing uses Form-Spree as a provider of contact form services. By submitting your personal data through our forms, you are agreeing that Form-Spree processes your data for the purpose of managing and sending messages to our team.

5. Limited Use and Disclosure:

Your personal data will only be used for the purpose of contacting you in response to your inquiries or requests. We will not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties for undisclosed or commercial purposes without your explicit consent.

6. Data Subject Rights:

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any concerns about how we process your data, please contact us through the contact information provided on our website.

Cheesing reserves the right to modify or update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of our website will constitute your acceptance of such changes.

We appreciate your trust in sharing your personal data with us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is handled securely and responsibly.
